The CHAMELEON network consists of 15 early stage researchers (ESRs). The Mental health representatives are responsible to gather mental health concerns of the ESR and bring important concerns to the attention of the supervisor. Furthermore, they organise regular mental health meetings and events to talk about and improve the mental health of the ESRs.
