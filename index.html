Hi, I'm Sven Kiefer, a PhD candidate in astrophysics.

I'm fascinated by exoplanets and love to study all aspects of them. Currently, I'm studying cloud formation in 3D exoplanet atmospheres. I try to deepen our understanding of how clouds on exoplants form, how they change the chemsitry and how they affect their climat. To this end, I implement kinetic cloud formation in general circulation models (GCMs) and analyse their feedback to the chemsitry and the climate. During my Bachelor and Masters, I focused more on the observational aspect of exoplanet science. I used a 0.5m telescope to prove that exoplanets can be found even in a larger city like Zurich. For my Master thesis, I implemented IFS to PynPoint to test the performance of differential imaging techniques. With it, new exoplanets can be searched in old and new observations.
